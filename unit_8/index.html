"And Then They Came"

By Hon. Roy W. King

Judge Roy W. King's new book, "And Then They Came", presents an accurate history of the contributions of the hundreds of African American lawyers who practiced law or were employed in law related careers in Monroe County, New York between 1899 and 2006.

All proceeds to benefit the Volunteer Legal Services Project

Judge Roy King culminated a distinguished law career as the Supervising Judge of the City of Rochester, NY Court, the Presiding Judge of the Drug Treatment Court, and as an Acting County Court Judge. Judge King was engaged in the general practice of law in Rochester for over 30 years. Currently, Judge King serves as Judicial Hearing Officer the New York Unified Court System's 7th Judicial District.
